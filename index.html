Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2026
BEENTA is a fully offline point-of-sale app. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers — everything stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers.

All data you enter — including products, sales records, transactions, and store settings — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Store name and configuration preferences
  • Product catalog and inventory data
  • Sales history and transaction records
  • Stock alert settings
  • In-app purchase unlock status

In-App Purchases

BEENTA offers optional in-app purchases (the "Premium Pack" and individual feature unlocks) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or process payment information directly. All payment data is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. Purchase unlock status is stored locally on your device.

Device Permissions

BEENTA may request access to:

  • Camera / Photo Library — to add product images. Images are stored locally on your device only.

We do not access your contacts, location, microphone, or any other device data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transmit your data to any third parties. Because the app operates entirely offline, no data is transmitted to us or any external service.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling BEENTA from your device.

Children's Privacy

BEENTA is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
